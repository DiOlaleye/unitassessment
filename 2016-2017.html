2016-2017 NBA season

The 2016-2017 season was a historic season for the entire NBA, as it was the first season after the reitrement of Kobe Bryant. The finals was expected to be a rematch of the previous year, with the Golden State Warriors facing off against the Cleveland Cavaliers. But this time, the Warriors were even more dominant, with the addition of Kevin Durant. They finished the regular season with a 67-15 record, and stormed through the west, going 12-0 in the playoffs to reach the finals.

The finals was a one-sided affair, with the Warriors winning the series and Kevin Durant being named Finals MVP for his outstanding performance throughout the series.

2017 NBA OFFSEASON

The biggests moves of the 2017 Offseason saw Paul George being traded to the Thunder, Kyrie Irving demanding a trade out of Cleaveland and being traded to the Boston Celtics, and Chris Paul being traded to the Houston Rockets.
